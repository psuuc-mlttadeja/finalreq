Welcome to dishCovery

Recipe recommendation engine and sharing platform

Filipino food is a vibrant and diverse cuisine that showcases a fusion of flavors influenced by Spanish, Chinese, and Southeast Asian cultures. It is characterized by bold and savory dishes such as adobo, a marinated meat stew, and sinigang, a sour soup made with tamarind. From crispy pork lechon to sweet and sticky halo-halo desserts, Filipino cuisine is a true reflection of the country's rich culinary heritage.

Filipino Food Recipes

"Experience the vibrant flavors of sinigang, a sour soup that is a staple in Filipino cuisine. Tangy tamarind, tender meat or seafood, and an assortment of vegetables come together in a comforting broth that is both refreshing and nourishing. This Filipino classic is a true celebration of the country's culinary heritage."

"Embark on a culinary adventure with our classic Filipino adobo recipe. Tender pieces of meat, simmered in a savory blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, create a dish that is both comforting and bursting with flavor. Serve it over steamed rice for a truly authentic Filipino dining experience."

"Transport your taste buds to the tropical islands of the Philippines with our refreshing and vibrant mango float dessert. Layers of sweet mangoes, graham crackers, and luscious whipped cream create a heavenly no-bake treat that is perfect for hot summer days or any occasion. Prepare to be enchanted by this delightful Filipino dessert."
