Corned Beef

Corned Beef Guisado is quick and easy to make yet full of flavor. It's a hearty and tasty dish that's perfect for breakfast or brunch!.

4 servings

Ingredients

    Meat

  • 1 can Style corned beef, chunky

    • Produce

  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Potato, large

    • Baking & Spices

  • 1 Salt and pepper

    • Oils & Vinegars

  • 2 tbsp Canola oil

    • Liquids

  • 1/2 cup Water
