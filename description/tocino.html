Homemade Pork Tocino

Pork Tocino is a Filipino dish cured in sugar, salt, pepper, and garlic. It's typically served with garlic fried rice and eggs for a delicious breakfast treat!

8 servings

Ingredients

    Meat

  • 2 lbs Pork butt

    • Produce

  • 1 tbsp Garlic powder

    • Baking & Spices

  • 4 cups Rice preferably, cooked long grain

    • Baking & Spices

  • 2 drops Food coloring, red
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 cup Sugar

    • Oils & Vinegars

  • 2 tbsp Canola oil

    • Liquids

  • 1 cup Water
