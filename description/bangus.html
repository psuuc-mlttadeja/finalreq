Daing na Bangus

Daing na bangus is a fried milkfish dish wherein the fish is marinated in a vinegar mixture and then fried until brown and crispy.

2 servings

Ingredients

    Produce

  • 1 head Garlic

    • Baking & Spices

  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp Peppercorn
  • 1/2 tsp Sea salt, coarse

    • Oils & Vinegars

  • 1/2 cup Cooking oil
  • 10 tbsp White or cane vinegar

    • Other

  • 1 Medium sized milkfish (cleaned and scales removed)
