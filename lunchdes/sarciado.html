Chicken Broccoli

8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tbsp Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 3 pcs Tomato
  • 1 cup Water
  • 3 pcs Egg
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 8 pcs Galunggong

Nutrional Information
Energy 179.53 kcal
Protein 13.2 g
Carbohydrates 9.48 g
Fats 9.97 g

Instructions

Step 1

Heat cooking oil. Sauté garlic, onion and tomatoes until fragrant and wilted. Pour in water and bring to boil.

Step 2

Add beaten eggs and continue stirring to scramble the eggs. Season with 8g MAGGI® Magic Sarap®.

Step 3

Add fried galunggong and simmer with the sauce for another minute. Serve.
