Tunasilog

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 2 cans Tuna
  • 1 pc Red Onion
  • 3 tbsp MAGGI® Savor Calamansi
  • 4 cups Cooked Rice
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 4 pcs Egg
  • 1 stalk Scallion

Nutrional Information
Energy 15.64 g
Carbohydrates 16.35 g
Fats 18.43 g

Instructions

Step 1

Sauté garlic in butter until light golden brown. Strain and set aside for topping.

Step 2

Add tuna in the same pan and cook for 2mins. Stir in onion and season with 2tbsp of MAGGI® Savor Calamansi. Remove and set aside.

Step 3

Add rice in the same pan. Season with remaining 1tbsp of MAGGI® Savor Calamansi and ¾ sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Transfer into serving plates and top with sautéed tuna.

Step 4

Fry eggs in vegetable oil until crispy. Season remaining ¼ sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap® Place on top of the rice.

Step 5

Top with scallion and toasted garlic. Serve immediately
