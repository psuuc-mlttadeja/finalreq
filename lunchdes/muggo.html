Ginisang Munggo

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup Munggog
  • 2.5 cups Water
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 2 pcs Cherry Tomato
  • 0.15 cup Shrimp
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 0.25 cup Malunggay

Nutrional Information

Instructions

Step 1

Simmer munggo in water while covered until tender.

Step 2

Sauté garlic, onion, tomato and hibe in oil. Add to simmering munggo. Simmer for 5mins.

Step 3

Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Stir in malunggay leaves. Transfer on serving bowl and serve.
