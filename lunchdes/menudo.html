Chicken Menudo

6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 pc Potato
  • 1 pc Carrot
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 0.5 kg Chicken Thigh
  • 1 pc Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 pc Green Bell Pepper
  • 200 g Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 pc Bay Leaf
  • 2 tbsp grated cheese
  • 2 sachets MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 0.25 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • NESTLÉ All Purpose Cream 125mlli

Nutrional Information
Energy 446.79 kcal
Protein 18.74 g
Carbohydrates 22.78 g
Fats 31.86 g

Instructions

Step 1

Sauté potato and carrot in oil until golden brown. Strain and set aside.

Step 2

Sauté garlic and onion in the same pan. Add chicken and bell peppers. Sauté for 5 minutes.

Step 3

Add tomato sauce, water and bay leaf. Stir and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 4

Add sautéed potato, carrot and cheese. Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®, pepper and sugar.

Step 5

Stir in Nestlé® All Purpose Cream. Simmer for another 2 minutes. Transfer into a serving plate and serve.
