Ginataang Sitaw Kalabasa, at Hipon

6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 1 pc Ginger
  • 1 pc Green Finger Chili
  • 0.5 cup Water
  • 2 cups Kalabasa
  • 2 cups Sitaw
  • 0.5 cup Coconut Cream
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 0.25 kg Shrimp

Nutrional Information
Energy 411.88 kcal
Protein 24.25 g
Carbohydrates 59.51 g
Fats 10.01 g

Instructions

Step 1

Sauté garlic, onion, ginger, finger chili in oil.

Step 2

Pour water, add kalabasa and simmer while covered for 10 minutes. Add sitaw, then simmer for another 2 minutes.

Step 3

Add coconut cream and shrimp. Simmer and season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
