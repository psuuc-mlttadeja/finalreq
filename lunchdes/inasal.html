Chicken Inasal

6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Kg Chicken
  • 2 Sachets MAGGI速 Magic Sarap速
  • 2 tbls Soy Sauce
  • 3 tbls White Vinegar
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 3 Stalks Lemongrass
  • 0.25 Cup Ginger
  • 2 tbls Brown Sugar
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 2 tbls Annatto Seeds
  • 0.25 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 6 Pieces Bamboo Shoots
  • 0.25 Cup Spiced Vinegar

Nutrional Information
Energy 1337.96 kcal
Protein 51.49 g
Carbohydrates 224.34 g
Fats 38.74 g

Instructions

Step 1

Marinate the chicken with MAGGI速 MAGIC SARAP速, MAGGI Soy, vinegar, garlic, lemongrass, ginger and sugar in the refrigerator overnight.

Step 2

Remove chicken from the refrigerator 1 hour before grilling. Skewer chicken in thick bamboo sticks.

Step 3

Remove chicken from the refrigerator 1 hour before grilling. Skewer chicken in thick bamboo sticks.

Step 4

Grill chicken over charcoal using low heat. Brush chicken with annatto oil. Transfer on a serving plate and serve with sinamak.
