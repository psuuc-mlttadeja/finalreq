Pork Caldereta

6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Kg Pork Spareribs
  • 2 Sachets MAGGI® Magic Sarap®
  • 2 tbls Vegetable Oil
  • 6 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Piece Onion
  • 1 Piece Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Piece Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Piece Bay Leaf
  • 0.5 Cup Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can Liverwurst Spread
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 2 tbls Brown Sugar
  • 0.5 Cup Coconut Cream
  • 0.25 Cup Cheese

Nutrional Information
Energy 564.4
Protein 43.49
Carbohydrates 33.72
Fats 24.47

Instructions

Step 1

Simmer munggo in water while covered until tender.

Step 2

Sauté garlic, onion, tomato and hibe in oil. Add to simmering munggo. Simmer for 5mins.

Step 3

Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Stir in malunggay leaves. Transfer on serving bowl and serve.
