Sinampalukang Adobo

4-6 servings

Ingredients

  • 0.5 Kg Pork Shoulder
  • 1 Head Garlic
  • 1 tsp Black Peppercorn
  • 3 tbls White Vinegar
  • 0.25 Cup Maggi® Soy Cooking And Seasoning Sauce
  • 1.25 Cups Water
  • 1 tbl Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Cups Water
  • 1 Piece Onion
  • 2 Pieces Tomato
  • 2 Pieces Gabi
  • 2 Pieces Finger Chili
  • 0.5 Cup Radish
  • 0.5 Cup Okra
  • 0.5 Cup Sitaw
  • 1 Sachet MAGGI® Magic Sinigang Sampalok With Gabi Mix
  • 1 Cup Kangkong

Nutrional Information
Energy 287.04 kcal
Protein 22.17 g
Carbohydrates 30.5 g
Fats 8.68 g

Instructions

Step 1

Sauté garlic, onion, ginger, siling panigang, pork belly and bagoong in oil. Pour vinegar and simmer for 2mins.

Step 2

Drain pork into a colander with a bowl and set aside to rest for 5 minutes. Separate pork from garlic and peppercorn.

Step 3

Heat oil in a separate pan, add pork and sauté for 3 minutes over medium heat. Add garlic and peppercorn and continue to sauté for another 2 minutes. Pour cooking liquid to the pan with pork and simmer for another 10 minutes while occasionally stirring . Remove and set aside.

Step 4

Combine water, onion, tomato and gabi in a pot. Bring to a boil and simmer while covered for 15 minutes. Mash the gabi to thicken the soup.Add finger chili, radish, okra and sitaw with 2-minute intervals. Pour MAGGI® MAGIC SINIGANG®. Stir in reserved adobo and kangkong. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve immediately.
