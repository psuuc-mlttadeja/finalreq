Chicken Curry

8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg Chicken Leg
  • 2.5 sachets MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 4 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 head Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 1 pc Potato
  • 1 pc Carrot
  • 1 pc Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 pc Green Bell Pepper
  • 0.5 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 1 pc Bay Leaf
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 package NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream

Nutrional Information
Energy 608.34 kcal
Protein 33.68 g
Carbohydrates 15.47 g
Fats 45.68 g

Instructions

Step 1

Season chicken with 2 sachets of MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®.

Step 2

Sauté garlic, onion, potato, carrot, and chicken in oil.

Step 3

Add bell peppers, curry powder, pepper, and bay leaf. Pour water and simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 4

Season with remaining ½ sachet of MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP® and stir in NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
