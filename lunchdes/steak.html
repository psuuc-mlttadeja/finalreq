Bistek-Style Pork Steak

6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pc Onion
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 0.5 kg Pork Shoulder
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Calamansi Juice

Nutrional Information
Energy 193.29 kcal
Protein 17.57 g
Carbohydrates 6.49 g
Fats 10.57 g

Instructions

Step 1

Sauté onion in oil. Remove and set aside.

Step 2

Season pork with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®. Sauté in oil and set aside.

Step 3

Sauté garlic in the same pan. Add pork, soy sauce, pepper sugar and water. Cover and simmer until tender.

Step 4

Stir in calamansi juice and simmer uncovered for another 10 minutes. Transfer into a serving plate and garnish with sautéed onion.
