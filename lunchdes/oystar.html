Oystar Mixed Vegetables

6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 head Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Root
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
    • Water>
  • 6 tbsp MAGGI® Oyster Sauce
  • 0.25 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 2 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 can Young Corn
  • 1 can Straw Mushroom
  • 1 head Broccoli
  • 1 head Cauliflower
  • 12 pcs Quail Egg

Nutrional Information
Energy 208.04 kcal
Protein 7.43 g
Carbohydrates 32.41 g
Fats 6.54 g

Instructions

Step 1

Sauté garlic, ginger, onion and bell pepper in oil.

Step 2

Pour water and MAGGI® Oyster Sauce. Season with pepper. Stir in cornstarch and water mixture to thicken the sauce.

Step 3

Add young corn, mushroom, broccoli, cauliflower and quail eggs. Gently toss. Transfer into a serving plate and serve.
