Nilagang Baka

8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Beef Short Ribs
  • 6 cups Water
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 2 pcs Bay Leaf
  • 1 tsp Black Peppercorn
  • 1 pc Yellow Corn
  • 1 pc Potato
  • 1 head Cabbage
  • 2 sprigs Spring Onion
  • 2 sachets MAGGI速 Magic Sarap速 8g

Nutrional Information
Energy 302.38 kcal
Protein 26.97 g
Carbohydrates 19.46 g
Fats 13.77 g

Instructions

Step 1

Simmer beef in water until slightly tender. Skim off fat as needed.

Step 2

Add onion, bay leaves, peppercorn. Simmer until beef is very tender.

Step 3

Add corn and simmer for 10 minutes. Add potato, cabbage and spring onion. Season with MAGGI速 Magic Sarap速. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve.
